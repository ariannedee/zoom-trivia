How it works

Before the game starts

  • Create your team or join an existing team below (refresh the page if you don't see your team)
  • Join the Zoom event and change your display name to your team name

How the game will work

  • There will be {{ game.rounds.count }} rounds. Each round (except lightning rounds), you will be put into breakout rooms while you answer the questions. We will use your display name to help sort you into rooms.
  • In this app, click the Start → button for the current round to go to the question page
  • Only one team member should fill in and submit the questions. They may want to share their screen so that others can see what has been answered.
  • The presenter will send a message to your breakout room when there is 1 minute left to submit answers and when time is up
  • After submitting, you can stay in your breakout room while the round gets marked
  • After marking, you will be brought back into the main group, go over the answers, then start the next round
